Il Padrone Fish House

The ‘Il Padrone Fish House’ is a high-end restaurant which is located very closely to Rabat’s town square.

As its name suggests, this restaurant primarily focuses on seafood. In fact, the extensive categories of fish it offers speaks for itself, which includes shell fish, marinated fish, and grilled fish.

Nevertheless, a customer of ‘Il Padrone Fish House’ could still order a pasta dish (all of which incorporate fish in some manner), or cut of beef if desired.

Veduta Restaurant

The ‘Il-Veduta’ restaurant offers a fine dining experience, with a scenic view like no other. In Maltese, ‘Il-Veduta’ directly translates to ‘the view’, showcasing the significance of the countryside that can be seen from the restaurant.

Other than the scenery, this restaurant prides itself on the vast quantity and sheer quality of its pizzas and pastas. However, ‘il-Veduta’ offers other selections of food such as salads, beef, chicken, fish, and omelettes. Not to mention that several menu items are labelled to be vegan, or gluten free.

Is-Serkin

Located at the heart of Rabat, ‘Is-Serkin’ restaurant offers an exceptional insight into Maltese food culture, at extremely affordable prices at that.

First and foremost, one is given the option try out this diner’s renowned ‘pastizzi’. ‘Pastizzi’ are by far the most notorious food item when it comes to Maltese food, which are basically pastries made out of puff pastry, with a filling of either ricotta or peas. Not to mention that this particular restaurant offers the additional ‘pastizzi’ filling of chicken.

Secondly, ‘Is-Serkin’ offers another Maltese food item called ‘qassatat’, which are best described as fancier variations of ‘pastizzi’ that are made out of a thicker dough, with a filling of either ricotta or peas. Yet again, this restaurant offers another filling for ‘qassatat’ which is that of anchovies.

Thirdly, if one is not in the mood for pastries, there is the option of ordering ‘ftajjar’ which are Maltese sandwiches.

Named after the breath-taking view that it presents, the ‘Point De Vue’ restaurant is one which provides palatable sustenance that comes with a moderate price.

‘Point De Vue’ provides a wide variety of meat dishes, which includes an array of burgers, pork, beef, chicken, fish, and rabbit. In fact, this restaurant’s menu has 4 pages which are solely dedicated to meat. If meat is not to your liking however, ‘Point De Vue’ also has a fine selection of pizzas, pasta, and salads to choose from.
